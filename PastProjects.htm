ICS4U - Deal With It ISP

This is the final project created by my friend Hannah and I, which has the theme "overcoming adversity". It is an interactive storyline game where the main character is a girl who is struggling with dealing with being bullied and peer pressured in her school. The objective of Level 1 (classroom) is to learn about the negative effects of peer pressure, which has always been a problem for teenagers, and Level 2 (gymnasium) covers strategies to defeat it. The main character must use these strategies to defeat peer pressure at the final stage (the party), and stand up for herself to defeat the bully.

Here is a link to the full code: Deal With It
